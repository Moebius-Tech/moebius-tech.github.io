# Nordea

1. Open the Nordea app or Nordea online banking
2. Press Pay and Transfer
3. Under account transfer, press Select bank
4. Scroll down and select Swedbank
5. Then enter account-clearing number 84202 8143866641, enter the name "Möbius" and click Continue
6. Select any amount, and then decide if you want to make a one-time donation or monthly donation
7. Check that everything is correct, and then confirm the payment. Thank you for your contribution! 🙂
