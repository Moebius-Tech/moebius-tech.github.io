# Danske Bank

1. Open the Danske Bank app or online banking, and go to pay
2. If you want to make a one-time donation, create a regular transfer. Otherwise, create a standing transfer by clicking the button under fixed payments
3. Press "+" to create a new standing transfer
4. Select "To others"
5. Enter amount, the name Möbius and account-clearing number 84202 8143866641
6. Press Next, and then select "Every month"
7. Press Next, and then go to confirmation
8. Check that everything is correct, and then approve the payment. Thank you for your contribution! :))
