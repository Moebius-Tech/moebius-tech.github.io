1) Open the Swedbank app or Swedbank online banking
2) Press Pay and Transfer
3) Press Pay and Transfer again
4) Press select recipient
5) Press the + button to add a new recipient
6) Select Bank account
7) Write Möbius and then write the account-clearing number 84202 8143866641
8) Select any amount, then decide if you want to make a one-time donation or monthly donation
9) Then add the payment; thank you for your contribution! :))
